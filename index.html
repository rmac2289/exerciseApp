NEVER MEAL PLAN ALONE AGAIN

Planning out your meals for the week is no fun - countless hours spent thinking about shopping and nutrition, often with a miscalculated end result. In comes the meal generator...

The meal generator will take your input and return breakfast, lunch, and dinner for 7 days. Click on the links and they'll take you straight to the recipes so you can get cooking!
All fields are optional - if you don't want to narrow it down, it will simply generate 7 days worth of random meals.

All out of an ingredient you need for a meal? See if there's a suitable substitute!
